    Inspiration And Implementation

    This is the Research page of the Web Site. In this page, We've outlined all the Websites that we investigated during the project for research purposes. These mentioned websites were taken as design reference and used to compliment our webpage's components to make the Website look more pleasing and user-friendly. All the websites components are compared and contrasted with the reference website's components below.

    1. HOME Page

    Designing the home page is crucial. First of all, we chose a background color that we thought would be suitable for our Printer Website. Then a easy to navigate navigation bar was necessary so we designed a navigation bar with black background, Logo on the left side and navigation options with bottom border hovers in the middle. Just like the image below. (Click on the image to go to the official website.)


    The Footer of the website includes 4 main columns. The first one consists of the terms and policy of our website. The second one consists of the number and Gmail of our website. The third one consists of the customer care and at last it contains the social media of our website.(Click on the image to go to the official website.)
